About Us

Homemaker.PK is a company that will help homeless people to build their own homes and provide projects in which even low-income people can fulfill their dream of owning a home.

Our Mission

We believe in teamwork, that the client is part of our team, his goal is our target and that we will accomplish this journey together. Our investment decisions are based on long-term payoff, and our fundamental beliefs are not sacrificed for short-term gain. This means we deliver more value than expected.

Our Vision

To be the only TRUE real estate agency that provides an excellent & consistent customer experience 100% of the time. We base every decision on what’s best for:– Our Clients,Our Company as a whole,Individual Team Members

About CEO

Mr. Ahmed Malik

Is CEO of Homemaker.Pk. I have a degree in business commerce. I also have law and political sciences degree, because of which I better understand the legal intricacies of business. I always prioritize business so that I may open a new path towards trading opportunities for others. My interest in construction enterprise is inherited from my father, who is in this profession for 40 years. I became part of this framework at the age of eighteen. Since then, I have sold hundreds of houses . Construction of a house employs forty factories and hundreds of people such as Engineers, Carpenters, Electricians, Plumbers, Painters, Masons, Laborers, Cement workers and so on. Hence by building houses, one beautifies the place and provide employment opportunities to many people. My aim is to make houses for those people who don’t have their own house.This website is a part of my aim. The people who want to own a house but dont know where to start, can access me through this website. Home is a basic need and right of human being. I am here to play my part for completion of this human right.

CEO Message

Home is a basic need of every human being. Even the animals and birds yearn towards framing a shelter. This basic urge is innate in them. Similarly, man should strive to build his nest as soon as possible. Homemaker.pk will help you concrete your dream nest as your own property.
